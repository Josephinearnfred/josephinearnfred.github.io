About Research Teaching

I’m a PhD Candidate at the Political Science Department at the University of Copenhagen, and I work to create knowledge on how to build more inclusive public institutions. Using experimental methods, I study how gender and ethnicity influence access to power — and explore strategies to make that access more equitable.
